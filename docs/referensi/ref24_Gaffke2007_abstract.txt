Judul  : Vector and matrix apportionment problems and separable convex integer optimization
Penulis: Gaffke, N.; Pukelsheim, F.
Jurnal : Mathematical Methods of Operations Research
Tahun  : 2007
DOI    : 10.1007/s00186-007-0184-7

ABSTRAK / KETERANGAN
----------------------------------------------------------------------
This paper extends the optimisation formulation of apportionment methods to vector and matrix allocation problems, proving that the divisor method framework applies beyond single-dimension proportional representation. The authors show that separable convex integer programs can be solved greedily using quotient-based priority rules — exactly the structure of the Sainte-Laguë scheduling algorithm. This provides the mathematical guarantee that the greedy Q(i) = wi/(2ni+1) selection rule converges to the global optimum proportional allocation.
