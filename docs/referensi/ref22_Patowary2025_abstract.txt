Judul  : Correctness of flow migration across Network Function instances
Penulis: Patowary, Ranjan; Barua, Gautam; Sukapuram, Radhika
Jurnal : Computer Communications
Tahun  : 2025
DOI    : 10.1016/j.comcom.2025.108284

ABSTRAK / KETERANGAN
----------------------------------------------------------------------
This paper formalises correctness conditions for flow migration in SDN-based network function virtualisation, establishing that policy transitions must maintain packet ordering and forwarding invariants throughout the update window. The work shows that naive flow migration strategies produce transient states where traffic is dropped or misrouted. This directly motivates the P5 concern: during the transition period after a load balancing weight change, distributional lag represents a form of policy inconsistency that Sainte-Laguë uniquely avoids.
