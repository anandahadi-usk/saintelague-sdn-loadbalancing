Judul  : Divisor methods for proportional representation systems: An optimization approach to vector and matrix apportionment problems
Penulis: Gaffke, Norbert; Pukelsheim, Friedrich
Jurnal : Mathematical Social Sciences
Tahun  : 2008
DOI    : 10.1016/j.mathsocsci.2008.01.004

ABSTRAK / KETERANGAN
----------------------------------------------------------------------
This paper establishes divisor apportionment methods as the solution to a convex integer optimisation problem, proving that methods like Sainte-Laguë uniquely minimise the maximum proportionality deviation across all possible weight vectors. The optimality guarantee provided here — that the Sainte-Laguë quotient Q(i) = wi/(2ni+1) greedily minimises proportionality error at each step — directly implies fast post-change convergence in the network scheduling context of the P5 research.
