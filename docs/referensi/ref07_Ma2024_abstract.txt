Judul  : Synchronizing DDoS detection and mitigation based graph learning with programmable data plane, SDN
Penulis: Ma, Jie; Su, Wei; Li, Yikun; Peng, Yihua
Jurnal : Future Generation Computer Systems
Tahun  : 2024
DOI    : 10.1016/j.future.2023.12.033

ABSTRAK / KETERANGAN
----------------------------------------------------------------------
This paper proposes a DDoS detection and mitigation framework that leverages programmable SDN data planes for real-time traffic engineering. The work demonstrates that SDN's separation of control and data planes, combined with programmable forwarding rules, enables dynamic reconfiguration of traffic routing policies without service interruption — a property central to the load balancing reconfiguration problem studied in the P5 project.
