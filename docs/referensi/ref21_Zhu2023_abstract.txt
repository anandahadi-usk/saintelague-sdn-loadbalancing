Judul  : FFRLI: Fast fault recovery scheme based on link importance for data plane in SDN
Penulis: Zhu, Zhengbin; Yu, Hong; Liu, Qinrang; Liu, Dongpei; Mei, Bo
Jurnal : Computer Networks
Tahun  : 2023
DOI    : 10.1016/j.comnet.2023.110062

ABSTRAK / KETERANGAN
----------------------------------------------------------------------
This paper proposes a fast fault recovery mechanism for SDN that prioritises critical links and avoids transient forwarding anomalies during rerouting. The work demonstrates that the period immediately following a network reconfiguration event — whether due to fault or policy change — is characterised by temporary routing inconsistencies that degrade application-layer performance. This is directly analogous to the distributional lag observed in P5 after weight change events, validating the practical importance of fast convergence.
