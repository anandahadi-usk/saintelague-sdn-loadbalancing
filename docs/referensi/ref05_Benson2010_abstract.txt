Judul  : Network traffic characteristics of data centers in the wild
Penulis: Benson, Theophilus; Akella, Aditya; Maltz, David A.
Jurnal : Proceedings of the 10th ACM SIGCOMM conference on Internet measurement
Tahun  : 2010
DOI    : 10.1145/1879141.1879175

ABSTRAK / KETERANGAN
----------------------------------------------------------------------
This paper presents a large-scale measurement study of traffic patterns in production data center networks. The authors collected and analysed traffic traces from multiple data centres and found highly skewed server utilisation distributions, with a small fraction of servers handling a disproportionate share of traffic — a finding that directly motivates the need for accurate proportional load balancing to avoid server saturation.
