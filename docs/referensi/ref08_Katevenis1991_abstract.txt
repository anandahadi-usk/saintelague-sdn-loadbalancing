Judul  : Weighted round-robin cell multiplexing in a general-purpose ATM switch chip
Penulis: Katevenis, M.; Sidiropoulos, S.; Courcoubetis, C.
Jurnal : IEEE Journal on Selected Areas in Communications
Tahun  : 1991
DOI    : 10.1109/49.105173

ABSTRAK / KETERANGAN
----------------------------------------------------------------------
This foundational paper formally analyses Weighted Round Robin (WRR) scheduling in the context of ATM switch chip design. The authors prove that WRR achieves perfect proportionality at cycle boundaries — that is, after every L = sum(wi) scheduling decisions, the allocation exactly matches the target weights. Between cycle boundaries, instantaneous MAPE oscillates as a function of the offset within the current cycle. This periodic oscillation property forms the theoretical basis for the 'Structural Lock' failure mode characterised in the P5 research.
