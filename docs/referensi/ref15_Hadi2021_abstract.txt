ABSTRAK / KETERANGAN
----------------------------------------------------------------------
This paper provides the most direct empirical baseline for the P5 research. The authors implemented WRR, IWRR, and WLC in a Mininet/OpenFlow testbed and measured MAPE, throughput, and latency under static weight configurations. Their results confirm that all three algorithms achieve near-perfect distribution in steady state, which aligns with the P5 S1 (steady-state) findings. Crucially, the Hadi et al. study used fixed weights throughout all experiments — the dynamic weight-change scenario that constitutes the core contribution of P5 was not evaluated. Published in Int. J. Intell. Eng. Syst., vol. 14, no. 3, pp. 422-432, Jun. 2021.
