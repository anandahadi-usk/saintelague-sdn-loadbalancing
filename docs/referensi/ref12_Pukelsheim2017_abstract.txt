ABSTRAK / KETERANGAN
----------------------------------------------------------------------
This comprehensive monograph provides the mathematical theory of proportional apportionment methods, including the Sainte-Laguë divisor method. Pukelsheim proves that divisor methods constitute the unique family of apportionment procedures that satisfy proportionality, monotonicity, and homogeneity simultaneously. The book establishes the theoretical optimality of Sainte-Laguë for unbiased seat allocation, providing the mathematical foundation for its application to proportional server load balancing in SDN environments.
