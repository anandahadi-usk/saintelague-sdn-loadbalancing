Judul  : A generalized processor sharing approach to flow control in integrated services networks: the single-node case
Penulis: Parekh, A.K.; Gallager, R.G.
Jurnal : IEEE/ACM Transactions on Networking
Tahun  : 1993
DOI    : 10.1109/90.234856

ABSTRAK / KETERANGAN
----------------------------------------------------------------------
This foundational paper establishes Generalized Processor Sharing (GPS) as the continuous-time ideal for weighted packet scheduling. The authors prove end-to-end delay bounds for GPS and show that any practical discrete scheduler approximating GPS achieves bounded deviation from ideal proportional allocation. WRR, IWRR, WF2Q, and the Sainte-Laguë method all constitute discrete approximations of GPS. This paper provides the theoretical context for evaluating convergence gaps in discrete weighted schedulers.
