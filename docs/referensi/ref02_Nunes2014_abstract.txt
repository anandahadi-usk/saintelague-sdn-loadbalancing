Judul  : A Survey of Software-Defined Networking: Past, Present, and Future of Programmable Networks
Penulis: Nunes, Bruno Astuto A.; Mendonca, Marc; Nguyen, Xuan-Nam; Obraczka, Katia; Turletti, Thierry

ABSTRAK / KETERANGAN
----------------------------------------------------------------------
This survey provides a comprehensive overview of the Software-Defined Networking (SDN) paradigm, covering its historical roots, current state, and future directions. The authors review the architectural principles that separate the control plane from the data plane, examine existing SDN implementations and programming interfaces, and identify open research challenges including runtime reconfiguration of forwarding policies.
